Hola/Hi/Greetings I am Asif , I love to wander around- from city to city from place to place . Without traveling I found it very difficult to breath. One more thing that helps me to breath is “Books”. Sometime in my lazy days I find it utmost peaceful to do a little bit research about philosophy and fine arts. My favorite philosopher is Rene Descartes for nothing but his one single line – “Cogito Ergo Sam” which literally opened the door of rational thinking for me and my favorite artist is Rene Magritte. Yes, another Rene. He is the reason why I find surrealism so awestrucking (no offence to Dali). Beside this I have a sickening weakness for movies. I don’t know why but movies of foreign languages gravitate me the most. My favorite director is Abbas Kiarostami. Mostly his “Taste of Cherry” and “Close-Up” (highly recommended if you haven’t watched these yet). Beside Kiarostami I love the works of Martin Scorsese and Stanley Kubrick.
